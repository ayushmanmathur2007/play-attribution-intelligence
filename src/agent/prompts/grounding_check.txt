## YOUR TASK
For EACH attribution claim, verify:

1. **Initiative exists**: If the claim references an initiative, verify it exists in the initiative calendar
2. **Date alignment**: Verify the initiative/event was active during the period in question
3. **Market match**: Verify the initiative targeted the relevant market
4. **Category match**: Verify the initiative targeted the relevant category
5. **Segment match**: Verify the initiative targeted the relevant segment
6. **Direction consistency**: If the initiative is designed to boost a metric, the attribution should show an increase, not a decrease
7. **Data support**: Verify the cited evidence is consistent with the actual data
